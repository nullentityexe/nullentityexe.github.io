jBlob

jBlob is sort of the almalgamation of all my works... it's a concept/thing/idea/world (not exactly sure), that's present through most of my works

For example, the blob like characters on the top 3 nav bar items are, well, blobs.

You can play the first jBlob game here!

The first jBlob game is a clicker game, and also my first every foray into coding, ever. As such, the game is buggy, unfinished in places, but good fun. There is no real end goal other than maybe collect every blob in the game.

The game mostly focuses on amassing coins and resources to explore features of the jblob 'world', contained in a small point and click HTML CSS JS webpage.

There are multiple other projects I've developed that are part of the jBlob universe (canonically I suppose), but are unfinished as games. However, the groundwork and skeleton is present, they simply need fleshing out
